## Project Overview

Welcome to the **NRF52 & SX1262 Module** project! In this project, the goal was to achieve dual-communication capabilities while drastically minimizing the physical footprint of the components. 

I successfully engineered the integration of both the robust **Nordic NRF52** MCU (renowned for its Bluetooth Low Energy processing) and the **Semtech SX1262** LoRa transceiver into one incredibly compact, unified PCB module.

![Module Layout View](/assets/img/projects/nrf52_sx1262_module/image.png)

## My Role: Hardware Design

For this project, I worked exclusively as the **Hardware Designer**. I architected the schematics and routed the printed circuit board from the ground up, tackling critical engineering challenges associated with high-frequency, complex RF routing:

![Module Detail and Footprint](/assets/img/projects/nrf52_sx1262_module/image1.png)

## Key Hardware Milestones
- **Core Processing**: NRF52 (MCU + BLE) combined with SX1262 (LoRa Transceiver)
- **Form Factor**: Castellated Half-Hole PCB layout for direct SMT integration
- **Signal Integrity**: EMI Shielding enclosure implementation

This project highlights my capability to lay out dense, high-frequency, production-ready RF modules perfectly tailored for modern IoT hardware ecosystems.
