Product Overview
Loupe is an ultra-compact wearable device designed with a
singular focus: maximum power efficiency in an extremely
small form factor. The project demonstrates DevBriX’s capability
to engineer reliable, sensor-accurate wearables under severe
size and energy constraints.
Our key
challenges
Extreme PCB miniaturization, limiting component
placement and routing flexibility
Maintaining sensor accuracy (heart rate & body
temperature) despite tight space and ultra-low
power operationDesigning Loupe required overcoming two major technical
bottlenecks: Balancing size, precision, and battery life
2-layer flex PCB, optimized for extreme size constraints
Epoxy conformal coating applied after final assembly for moisture
and durability protection
 Ultra-small 3.7V Li-Po battery
Magnetic pogo-pin connector for compact and reliable charging
